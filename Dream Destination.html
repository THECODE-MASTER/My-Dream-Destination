My Dream Destination


My Dream Destination is singapore though I have been there 2 times but I still like that place. Singapore is very eco-friendly and while being eco-friendly is very ahead in technology. They integrate nature in every thing they build. A good example is garden by the bay. They have a good amount of automation and have strict laws regarding smoking and other things. It is a very nice place and it has good architecture and produces a variety of interesting products.
